%% Content %%

The Riemann $\xi$-function, defined by the formula
\[
\label{1}
  \xi(iz)
  =
  \frac12 \left(
    z^2 - \frac14
  \right) \pi^{-\frac{z}{2}-\frac14} \Gamma \left(
    \frac{z}{2} + \frac14
  \right) \zeta \left(
    \frac12 + z
  \right)
\tag{1}
\]
